About BuzzTalk

BuzzTalk is an exclusive chatroom platform for students. It provides a secure and interactive space for users to connect, collaborate, and share ideas. If you want to message a person privately, just click on their name to copy their email.

Data Collection and Usage

To provide its services, this website collects and stores the following information in the Firebase console:

  • Email Address: Used for user identification and communication.
  • Name: Displayed alongside your messages for identification.
  • Message Content: The messages you send in the chatroom.

This data is used solely for enhancing the user experience within BuzzTalk and will not be shared with any third parties.
